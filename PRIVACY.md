# Privacy Policy

**cmux-mcp** does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information.

## Data Handling

- All commands and terminal output are processed locally on your machine.
- No data is sent to external servers, analytics services, or third parties.
- No telemetry, tracking, or logging of user activity.
- Communication between cmux-mcp and cmux happens entirely via local Unix socket.

## MCP Protocol

cmux-mcp communicates with MCP clients (e.g., Claude Code) over stdio. The content of these communications — including commands you run and terminal output — is handled by the MCP client according to its own privacy policy. cmux-mcp itself does not intercept, store, or forward this data.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, open an issue at [github.com/daegweon/cmux-mcp](https://github.com/daegweon/cmux-mcp/issues).

This policy may be updated. Changes will be reflected in this file and committed to the repository.
